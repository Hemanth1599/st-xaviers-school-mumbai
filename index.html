East of Gaffarbeg Street in Pune Cantonment, stands the Stately Shivaji Market. 'Stately' does not mean clean, though. An overpowering stench and ever-present filth are the hallmarks of the Contonment's principal market complex, especially meat and fish. Birds of prey circle the area, feasting on the little flesh hat spills out of garbage bins or shops. Trucks and people throng the place furiously , honking or cussing with impatient abandon.

Achievements

  • INDIAN NATIONAL CARTOGRAPHIC ASSOCIATION’S MAP QUIZ 2019

    Event date : 20th Dec, 2019 The 39th Indian National Cartographic Association’s International Congress on the theme “ New Age Cartography & Geospatial Technology in Digital India” was held from 18th to 20th December 2019 at Hotel Sun Park Inn Dehradun Uttarakhand. A team from Saint Xavier’s High School Patna took part in the 39th Inca National Map Quiz & stood 2nd at the National level. The team was guided by Dr. Shahla Kauser. The team comprised of:- 1- JAINENDRA NARAYAN SINGH, ‘ X C ’ 2- MAYANK ANAND, ‘ X C ’ 3- ABDUS SAMAD , ‘ X C ’ Jainendra Narayan Singh of class ‘ X C ’ represented the team at 39th Inca International Congress accompanied by Dr. Shahla Kauser, the concerned Geography teacher. The team awards was given by His Excellency Admiral General Vinay Badhwar, AVSM, PVSM, Chief Cartographer to the Government of India, Lieutenant General Girish Kumar, Suryeyor General of India & President INCA & Dr. V C Jha, Ex Vice - Chancellor, Bhagalpur University. JAINENDRA NARAYAN SINGH was the one and only student who got the golden opportunity to give vote of thanks at the event.



What's new


  • The Library

    Our library’s objective is to inculcate the habit of reading; children are introduced to the library and books right from the Kindergarten level. They realize the joys of reading as well as the importance of books, in their formative years itself. Reference books are also kept to help students with their course studies. The cataloging and issue procedure in the library is through library cards. Students at St. Xaviers are encouraged not only to pursue reading but also to develop the art of story-telling, enacting and reading aloud.

  • Computer Room

    At St. Xaviers the emphasis is on quality education and this tradition is reflected very clearly in our Computer Department also. We have made it a point to keep our children abreast with the fast paced changes in the computer world, rather than making them follow the stereo-type computer syllabus.  We want to nurture creative and thinking minds that are forever exploring new realms in the computer arena.

  • Play Grounds

    Open space, fresh air, voices cheering and spirits soaring is what you will see on our playgrounds. These days with little or no play area and time as well; we have ensured that our children do not miss out on it at school also. We have a huge play area which accommodates a football field.
